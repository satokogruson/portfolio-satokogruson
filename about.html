About Me

Satoko Gruson - UI/UX Designer & Frontend Developer

I am a UI/UX designer and aspiring frontend developer based in Munich, Germany, with a rich multicultural background and over 10 years of corporate experience. Having lived and worked in Canada, Japan, Australia, and Germany, I bring a unique perspective to design and team management.

Throughout my career, I have honed skills in cross-functional team management, project planning, and cultural nuances, positioning me uniquely within the UI/UX landscape, especially in MedTech where I aim to integrate design and development principles to enhance user experiences.

With over a decade in leadership roles, I excel in managing diverse teams and complex projects, ensuring seamless integration and timely delivery.
